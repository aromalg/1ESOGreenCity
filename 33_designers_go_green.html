3.3. Designers go green

Apps and platforms

Use these apps and platforms to create your digital city:

Licenses

In order to design your green city, you need images: recycling containers, solar panels, houses, school, museum, etc. But be careful! They need to be copyright-free. Images online have an author, and they freely decide if they want to share it with you, if you can use it or change it. So, how do you know the type of license they have got? Look at this chart:

Image browsers

Now that you know that not all the images are free, here you have a link to browse and find the correct one for you.

Canva

Genial.ly

This platform can create interactive presentations, so you can add not only images but links to videos and audios you can create.
